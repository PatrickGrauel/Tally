Design notes

Squircle. Continuous-curvature superellipse with n=5, generated as a 256-segment closed path centred at (512, 512), radius 512. Matches the macOS Sonoma+ icon mask closely. Glyph art sits inside a ~736×736 safe area (~144px inset on each edge), well clear of the corner curves.

Σ glyph. Polyline through (256,254) → (706,254) → (322,512) → (706,770) → (256,770), stroked at 84px (≈8.2% of canvas width) with miter joins and miterLimit=10. Bounding box 450×516 — taller than wide (aspect 0.87), the proportion that reads as Σ rather than as a sideways hourglass. The 70° interior angle at the apex produces a ~146px miter spike extending left to x≈176 — that sharp left point is the Σ's defining silhouette and is what survives 16px. Apex at (322, 512), vertically centred, horizontally biased so the open right side has room for the horizon. Bounding-box centre at x=481, ~31px left of canvas centre — intentional; the rightward horizon visually rebalances.

Horizon line. Stroked from the apex (322, 512) to (870, 512) at 16px, orange @ 72% opacity, round caps. It crosses the Σ's open right side, so it reads as a continuation of the apex outward — not a separate line tacked on. Terminated with a 20×112px rounded vertical bar at x=860–880 in full-opacity orange. The bar is the "tick that hints at an aircraft silhouette" from the brief, abstracted to a heading-marker pip — no literal plane.

Why this wins at 16×16. Concept 2's heading bug becomes a 1px triangle (0.5px wide at base) — gone. Concept 3's cardinal ticks merge into the ring's stroke and the italic ƒ collapses into a vertical sliver. Σ has no fine detail — four straight segments and one sharp apex spike. At 16×16 the stroke anti-aliases to a 1.4px-wide orange wash that still reads as "Σ on dark"; at 32×32 the horizon starts to appear faintly; from 64×64 up the full mark is present. Graceful degradation, not failure.

Pixel-snapping advice. All ten sizes are rasterised independently at native resolution from the same 1024-coord drawing — no downsampling artefacts. If after build you notice the 16×16 looking too washy in Finder list views, regenerate just that size with stroke 104 and inset the apex to (320, 512) — the small tightening makes the diagonals snap to whole-pixel widths. The 32×32 generally lands clean as-is. The horizon at 16×16 will read as a faint smear rather than a line; that's the intended fall-back.

Optical tuning. No gloss, no inner shadow, no rim light — quiet by design. The background is a flat linear gradient top-to-bottom (#0B1320 → #1A2B47) plus a barely-there radial highlight at cy=0.2 × canvas, white @ 4%, fading to 0 by 70% radius. That single ambient lift gives the icon depth in the Dock without entering glossy-plastic territory.
